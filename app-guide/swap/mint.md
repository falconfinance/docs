# Mint

## **Supported Collateral Types**

**Stablecoins:**

* **USDT**
* **USDC**&#x20;
* **FDUSD**&#x20;

**Non-Stablecoin Assets:**

* **BTC**
* **ETH**&#x20;
* **TON**
* **NEAR**
* **XRP**&#x20;
* **DEXE**
* **BEAMX**
* **MOVE**
* **POL**
* **EOS**
* **TRX**&#x20;
* **SOL**
* **FET**&#x20;
* **COTI**

_For any other tokens, please reach out to support on falcon.finance for assistance._

## Steps - Minting with Stable coins

### **1. Navigate to Mint Section:**&#x20;

* Access the "Mint" area on the app.​

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (11).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

### **2. Enter Minting Amount**

* Specify the **amount of Stable coins** ( USDT, USDC, or FDUSD)  you want to use for minting **USDf**.

_Example:_

* _Minting USDf with 10,000 USDT._

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (12).png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Enter amount of USDT with USD  Value, with Balance showing.</p></figcaption></figure>

### **3. Confirm Minting**

* Authorize the minting process and approve the transaction in your wallet.

_Example:_

* _Using MetaMask as an example wallet to confirm the request_

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (13).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

### 4. Minting Request Success Notification

* Once the minting request is successfully submitted, you will receive a **notification** within the Falcon App.&#x20;

_Example:_

* _Mint request of **10,000 USDT** submitted, showing a balance of **40,000 USDT** (50,000 - 10,000)._

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (14).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

### **5. Receive USDf**&#x20;

* The minted USDf will appear in your wallet upon confirmation.​

_Example:_

* _**Minted USDf:**_ _**9,990 USDf** based on the deposit of 10,000 **USDT**._

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (15).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

## **Minting with Non-Stablecoins (**&#x65;.g., BTC, ET&#x48;**)**

### **1. Navigate to Mint Section:**&#x20;

* Access the "Mint" area on the Falcon App.​

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (11).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

### **2. Enter Minting Amount & Review Collateralization Ratio**

* Specify the **amount of Non-Stablecoins** you want to use for minting **USDf**.
* Review the current OCR before submitting a minting request.

_**\*OCR may change based on market conditions, asset volatility, and other factors.**_&#x20;

_Example:_

* _Falcon app will calculate the amount of **USDf** you can mint based on the current market value of **ETH** and the **Overcollateralization Ratio (OCR)**._
  * _**OCR:**_ _**2%** (meaning 2% **Overcollateralization Ratio** is required)_
  * _**OCR buffer amount:**_ _**0.1 ETH**_
  * _**Market Value:** Current **ETH market value** is **$1,938.51 per ETH**_
* _**Calculation:**_
  * _**5 ETH deposit** is worth **$9,692.58**_
  * _**Amount available for minting USDf:** value of used ETH - 2% OCR_
  * _The_ Falcon App _will mint **9,498.94 USDf** based on the excess collateral and market value of ETH._
  * _The **2% OCR buffer** will be **locked** and can be **claimed when you claim the locked position by returning enough USDf**. (Detailed in_[  ](claim-locked-positions.md)_Claim (Locked_ [_Positions_](claim-locked-positions.md)_))_

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (16).png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Enter amount of ETH with USD Value, &#x26; Review OCR</p></figcaption></figure>

### **3. Confirm Minting**

* Authorize the minting process and approve the transaction in your wallet.

_Example:_

* _Using MetaMask as an example wallet to confirm the request_

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (17).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

### **4. Minting Request Success Notification**

* Once the minting request is successfully submitted, you will receive a **notification** within the Falcon App.&#x20;

_Example:_

* _Mint request of 5 ETH submitted, and showing Balance of 5 ETH (10-5)._

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (18).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

### **5. Receive USDf & Locked Position**

* The minted USDf will appear in your wallet upon confirmation.​

_Example:_

* _**Minted USDf:**_ _**9,498.94 USDf** based on the deposit of **5 ETH**._
* _**Locked Position:**_ _**5 ETH** with a **2% OCR** (**Overcollateralization Ratio) , 0.1 ETH** as OCR Buffer Amount._

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (19).png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Receive "Mint Complete" Notification &#x26; USDf in Your "Connected Wallet"</p></figcaption></figure>

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (20).png" alt=""><figcaption><p><em><strong>Locked Position:</strong></em> <em><strong>5 ETH</strong> with a <strong>2% OCR</strong> (<strong>Overcollateralization Ratio) , 0.1 ETH</strong> as OCR Buffer Amount.</em></p></figcaption></figure>

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (21).png" alt=""><figcaption><p><em><strong>Minted USDf:</strong></em> <em><strong>9,498.94 USDf</strong></em></p></figcaption></figure>
